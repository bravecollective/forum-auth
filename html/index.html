FAQ
How does this work?

By clicking the "Update from Core" button your character has been created as a forum user.
Click the "Reset Password" button to generate a new random password, then use the credentials listed on the left in order to login. Be aware that you can change your password in the forum itself or use this page to generate a new random password anytime.

First Steps?

Once you successfully logged into the forum, you should setup your profile.
Adding your email and jabber address to your profile is recommended as they can be used for notifications in case you receive a PM or are quoted in a reply.

What about my Alt Characters?

The user under which you are known on the forum is your Character as shown on the left.
For each associated character in Brave Core, your character inherits the permissions from that character (e.g. access to corp forums in case they exist).
